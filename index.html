aura is a start-up company that is focused on developing applications that will open augmented reality to the masses and seamlessly allow communication between the physical and virtual worlds. Our team is comprised of business and research professionals as well as software and hardware developers. We believe that AR/VR has a bright future, but in order to unlock that future, technologies have to be simple to use, easy to distribute, and computationally efficient. The products that our team are building do just that.
At aura our goal is to make technology easy to use and seamlessly integrate the physical and virtual worlds. The technology stack that we have created is extremely versatile. Apps based on our technology have applications in many different fields: Safety and Security - best in class 3D positional data, in real time, even in locations with low cellphone coverage. Parks, Zoos, and Museums - interactive tours that can be experienced in both the real and virtual worlds. Everyone Else - let your imagination run wild with a simple web interface that makes it easy to create content and augment the world around you.
